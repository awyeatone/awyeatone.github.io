Doug Lipstick: Doug Lipstick

Biography

From the moment this adorable infant first grasped his mothers long squiggly wet fingers so many years ago, Doug Lipstick has always been fascinated by eels. From young eel-firehose firefighter to surrogate whisperer of bad news, Lipstick is truly the R'eel D'eel. Doug Lipstick embodies the values and skills of a bonified Eel Master. His magnum opus, Doug Lipstick's All-Eel Zoo is an amazing place. With lots of eels, and no other animals. Eels only. Just the way Doug likes it.
